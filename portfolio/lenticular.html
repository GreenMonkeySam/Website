Lenticular

Infinite Lotus - Software Solutions

A community driven map tagging platform to find and share the hidden gems around you.


Abstract


Trusted maps are great when you're looking for well-known establishments, like businesses or schools or events. But how many times have you had trouble finding a clean park? How many times have you found outdated information from a closed down store? With Lenticular, the community keeps the map updated with the best information about their local area. Great for visiting or exploring your surrounding.

The locals always know the best place to eat, play, and explore. That's why we created a platform for sharing just that! Tag areas on the map to help others find the hidden gems around you. Great parks to walk your dog. The best food that might be a bit hidden. The faraway hiking path with the perfect view. Whatever it is, you can share it with the rest of the community.

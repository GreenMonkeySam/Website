Abstract


Who has the family car? Where is your roommate? What're your friends up to this weekend? Are your students ready for their presentations? Check out their Persona and keep in the loop!

Organize groups, add others, and keep in the loop with what everybody is up to. Helpful for projects, teams, families, and friends to keep in touch and keep the fun moving. Great for teachers, professors, and TAs to keep track of their students. Perfect for gamers looking for group. Easy for families to keep track of each other. Fun for friends to share their plans. Persona is made for keeping in touch and sharing your plans. So get together and share your Persona!

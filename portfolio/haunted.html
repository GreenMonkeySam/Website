Haunted

Mage Youth Inc.

You've just died. What an inconvenience. Time to take it out on the living.


Description


In Haunted, you are a recently deceased spirit. To sustain yourself in the spirit world and avoid moving on, you must haunt people and feed on their fear. Literally. Fear keeps you tethered to the spirit world and gives you amazing abilities. As you progress you get more powerful and become a malevolent spirit wishing to cause global fear to become a legend.

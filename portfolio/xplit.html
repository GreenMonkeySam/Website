Xplit

Infinite Lotus - Software Solutions

An easier way to split the bill and reimburse your friends.


Abstract


There's seven people, you all shared an app, drinks we're half off but not everyone drank, the couple is paying together, oh...and you still owe your best friend from last time! How do you split the bill? Easy! Just Xplit it!

An easier way to track the bill or other expenses between friends, family, and acquaintances. Keep track of who owes what. Pay a single bill. Then take care of the rest with Xplit it. Hassle free, documented, and even with personalized receipts for each party! Xplit is the easiest way to keep track of who owes what and an even easier way to reimburse each other for bills of the past. Connect with your financial accounts and connect with friends and never lose track of the tab again! Great for personal or business use.

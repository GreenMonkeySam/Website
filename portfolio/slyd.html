Infinite Lotus - Software Solutions

Cross-platform copy and paste.


Abstract


Found a great article on your phone that you want to save for reading at home? Need to send a screenshot of your laptop to one of your phone contacts? We've all been there. You find something you want to share with someone else or one of your other devices but it becomes a chore. There are too many steps between finding and transferring data between devices, even for something as simple as a hyperlink!

With Slyd you can easily copy and paste between devices! as easily as you can copy and paste now. With Slyd installed on your devices, your menus will now include "Slyd Copy" and "Slyd Paste". These options work the same way they do now but between all devices with Slyd! You can even Slyd between accounts for easier sharing.

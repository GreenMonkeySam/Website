Mage-Ring

Mage Youth Inc.

A living deck building game.


Description


One deck, multiple players. Inspired by games like Magic and Ascension. You start with a deck of only ten cards. They are used to purchase spells from a mutual shop. Once purchased they are added to your deck. Then all you have to do is defeat your opponents before they defeat you!

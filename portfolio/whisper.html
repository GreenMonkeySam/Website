Whisper

Noble Crest - Software Security

Manage your digital assets with the automatic digital will.


Abstract


Your will handles your personal assets and ensures your last requests are fulfilled after you pass, but what about your digital assets? Whisper is a digital will for your digital presence. Close accounts, post messages, delete files & history, and automatically manage your digital life. Grant yourself the peace of mind knowing your digital presence will be taken care of the way you want after you're gone.

Setup your account and attach any online accounts you have. Then tell us exactly how you would like your digital assets handled and authorize the requests through your lawyer. Delete files, history, accounts, comments and more; online or offline on your personal devices. You can even create posts and time-delay them for warm messages to leave behind for your loved ones. However you want, whatever you need, leave your final wishes to Whisper.

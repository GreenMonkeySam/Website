Trade-Net

Infinite Lotus - Software Solutions

The ever-open community market in your pocket.


Abstract


Trade goods and services between local community members in a breeze. Let Trade-Net be the online market, accounting, tracking, and advertising in one app! Great for farmers' markets, garage sales, bake sales, local communities, and any other trading service you can offer.

Setup your personal store in minutes. Advertise to your local community. Set the trade prices, whether in cash, credit, or a trade. Set your own hours. Automatically manage your inventory or services. Rate others stores and share recommendations. Grow your community and bring the market back to the people!

Pokémon GO Game Redesign


Description


Many long time Pokémon fans thought that Pokémon GO was lacking in many places. Here's how one fan would merge the Pokémon world with our world. I've taken the concept of Pokémon GO and reimagined it to be more like the Pokémon games everyone remembers; and the one most fans were hoping Pokémon GO would be.





I'm currently in the process of compiling my work and assets as well as getting the proper permissions to show this work.

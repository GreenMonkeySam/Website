The Mana Pool

Various Parties

A website for the MtG community.


Abstract


Magic:The Gathering is a popular TCG with an incredible numbers of gamers all over the world. With so many ways to play and even more things to do between games, Magic is a vast universe that can get very complex at times; even when you're not playing!

The Mana Pool is a place where the MtG community can come hang out, discuss the meta-game, build, play, share, and grow. The vast website has the features that players want but are currently scattered across the web: set spoilers, card power-ratings, videos, articles, pod-casts, community content, story, weekly features, deck building, sealed pool generator, draft simulator, and so much more!

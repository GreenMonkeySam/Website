Gawker

Noble Crest - Software Security

Your smart-device can be your personal security in an emergency.


Abstract


In times of emergency, you only have seconds to react. Gawker uses the hardware in your smart-device as triggers to an emergency situation. Using the accelerometer, GPS, gyroscope, and digital data; Gawker can correctly asses an emergency situation and take the action you've arranged beforehand.

Our smart-devices are multi-tools that make life easier. Now it makes life safer too. Gawker is a personal security application designed to react quickly and accordingly to emergency situations. Gawker can be setup to automatically contact your trusted-ones in times of emergency, call the proper authorities, automatically take sound and/or video recordings and document as many details as possible at the times of the event such as day; time; weather; location, and more.

ReQuest

Infinite Lotus - Software Solutions

Make the party the DJ.


Abstract


Being the DJ of a party is a full time job and every DJ knows how many annoying requests you get from everyone at the party. Now you can let ReQuest manage all of the music requests!

Propose songs from any platform: Spotify, YouTube, iTunes, Google Play, or whichever music software you use! Vote other songs up or down. Based on their rating they'll go up or down in the queue and maybe even get removed altogether. Make the party the DJ, and let the DJ enjoy the party!

Fracas

Mage Youth Inc.

A MOBA unlike any other. Inspired by the Magic universe.


Description


A multi-player online battle arena. Five players enter with no items, skills, or unique abilities. They must explore the large map while drawing mana from their surroundings. Summon creatures to do your bidding. Cast devastating spells. Evoke enchantments upon the world. Materialize artifacts to help your in your goal of victory. Exploring the land awards you with new spells. Find and defeat your four opponents and be the last one standing. Join the Fracas.

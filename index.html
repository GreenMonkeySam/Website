Samir Aryan


I'm an enthusiastic developer & designer. I have an obsession with technology and aesthetic. I'm known to have a creative mind with endless ideas and innovative solutions. I'm constantly learning and updating a wide range of skills in software development & design. I'm inquisitive and always looking for a new adventure!

My background in Software Engineering and development, along with my passion and skill in design, gives me an edge in UX Design. I can see software from both perspectives. The technical side; which is concerned with data management, software flow, error-avoidance, and smooth operation; and the design side; which is focused on user-friendliness, approachability, efficiency, and simplicity.

My design principles are straightforward: user-centered minimalist design, incorporation of game elements in UX, and no-friction software flow. I'm currently working with some start-ups to get their ideas from paper to development. Sometimes I like to stick around and consult throughout the development, the launch, and/or post-launch. I specialize in UI design and user interaction.

The software I've been designing for has mostly been mobile applications. However, as any good UX designer, I have experience in many fields. From digital marketing, to cryptocurrency.

I have a wide range of skills, so I often spend my time in many creative yet productive ways. From graphic and UI design, front-end development, web development, animation, software design, game design, to video production, film, and directing.

I'm always asking questions, wondering how to make things better, cutting out the clutter, refining to efficiency, and shaping the digital world into a place for everyone.

Interested in working together? I'm based in the GTA, so you can usually find me in Toronto or Mississauga. Although I've worked with people from Waterloo, Kitchener, Oakville, Markham, and the surrounding areas.
